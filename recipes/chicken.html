How to Make Garlic Parmesean chicken

Description

A quick and crispy baked chicken anyone can make with just a few handy ingredients.
This garlic parmesean chicken recipe makes for great leftovers (if there is any leftover!)
Pair with the How to Make Basic Mashed Potatoes recipe also linked in this mini cookbook for an easy yet delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 2/3 clove garlic, minced
  • 2/3 cup dried bread crumbs
  • 1/3 cup and 1 1/2 tablespoons as well as an extra 2 teaspoons of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teasppon dried basil leaves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F and lightly grease a 9x13 in baking dish
  2. In one bowl, blend the olive oil and garlic. In another, mix the bread crumbs, parmesan, basil, and pepper.
  3. Dip each chicken breast half first in the oil mixture and then into the bread crumbs before placing them in your baking dish.
  4. Top with the remaining bread crumb mixure before baking for 30 min or until the juices run clear and the meat is no longer pink.
