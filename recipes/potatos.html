How to Make Basic Mashed Potatoes

Description

If you like a classic potatoe recipe then you'll love our Basic Mashed Potatoes!
These light and fluffy potatoes pair perfectly with our How to Make Baked Garlic Parmesan Chicken recipe.
To make a creamy yet delicate side that will surly complete any meal just follow these few simple steps listed below.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds baking potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 clove garlic
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil and add a small amount of salt
  2. Add your quartered potatoes and cook until slightly firm, this should take around 15 min.
  3. Drain your potatoes and set them aside
  4. Now in a small saucepan heat butter, garlic, and milk over low heat until the butter has melted and the garlic becomes fragrant.
  5. Using a potato masher, slowly combine the milk mixture into the potatoes until smooth.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
